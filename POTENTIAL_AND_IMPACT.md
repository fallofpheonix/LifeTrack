# Potential and Impact

## User Impact
- Better adherence through consolidated workflows.
- Improved trend awareness for chronic-condition users.
- Reduced friction in preparing data for medical consultations.

## Technical Potential
- Existing modular architecture supports incremental vertical features.
- Encryption/governance primitives enable safer expansion.
- Seeder and provider/store tests support rapid iteration and regression control.

## Business/Product Potential
- Can evolve into premium analytics, clinician integration, or care plans.
- Strong candidate for partnerships with wellness providers and telehealth ecosystems.

## Constraints and Risks
- Medical-claim boundaries must stay explicit.
- Data quality and missing-input issues can degrade insight quality.
- Sync/conflict complexity increases sharply with multi-device workflows.
